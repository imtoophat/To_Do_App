# About Our App: As the world advances in technology and time starts becoming more valued, we wanted to bring to our users a way to track their valuable time in their very busy schedules. This To-Do Tracker App allows our user to track the tasks they have and how much time they have spent on it. This allows for better time management and a tool to help with managing your time.#
